About the Initiative

Combating Garbage Accumulation for a Healthier City

Our municipality is dedicated to maintaining the highest sanitation standards. However, unscheduled garbage dumping in residential areas and public places often stays unattended between weekly collection schedules, giving rise to toxic odors, insect infestations, and health risks.

The Smart Waste Management System offers an immediate, digital direct-response platform. Citizens can photograph illegal dump locations on their phones, and our system automatically appends GPS coordinates, alerting sanitation officers and dispatching cleaning teams immediately.

Features & Services

How We Keep the City Clean

Image Upload

Take a photo of the accumulated waste. Images verify the complaint scope and allow workers to prepare correct cleaning tools.

GPS Location Capture

HTML5 Geolocation API maps precise coordinates (latitude & longitude) automatically, leading workers straight to the garbage spot.

Complaint Tracking

Citizens can log in to view historical submissions, status updates, and review remarks from supervisors and cleaners.

Fast Municipal Response

Real-time dashboards allow administrators to monitor newly reported waste items and authorize action immediately.

Worker Assignment

Admins assign cleaning work directly to localized field workers. Workers check tasks on their phone-friendly dashboards.

Before & After Verification

Completed tickets require workers to upload an 'After Cleaning' photo, ensuring the spot is clean before closure.

Quick Guide

System Workflow

1
Report Waste

Citizen logs in, uploads waste image. Coordinates are captured automatically.

2
Admin Review

Municipal admin inspects complaint, checks details, and assigns a local worker.

3
Worker Dispatch

Worker navigates to GPS location, marks status as 'In Progress', and clears trash.

4
Close Ticket

Worker uploads 'After Cleaning' photo and marks complete. Citizen views update.
